Current Ph.D. Topic: Pattern Effect and Precipitation Response

My Ph.D. focuses on the Pattern Effect, which examines how different spatial patterns of SST warming influence climate feedbacks and tropical precipitation. This research helps explain why precipitation changes can vary despite similar overall warming levels.

M.Sc. Dissertation: Predicting Cyclones over Bay of Bengal

For my M.Sc., I developed statistical and simple machine learning models to predict seasonal cyclonic activity over the Bay of Bengal. This involved identifying climate predictors linked to cyclone frequency to improve seasonal forecasts.
